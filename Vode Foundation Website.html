VODE Foundation

Empowering youth through education and opportunity in Ghana

About Us

The VODE Foundation is a youth-focused non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering young people in Ghana through formal and informal education, vocational training, and leadership development programs. We believe every young person deserves the tools and opportunities to succeed and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Programs

Vocational Training

Hands-on training in skills such as tailoring, carpentry, and technology to equip youth for employment.

Youth Leadership

Workshops and mentorship to build confidence, entrepreneurial skills, and community engagement.

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, and tutoring programs to support academic success for young learners.

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) Training & Mentorship

Providing hands-on training and mentorship in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), including coding, robotics, digital skills, and innovation to prepare youth for future opportunities.
